An Open Letter to Technology Companies

Join the fight against mass surveillance before it's too late.

The undersigned organizations and inviduals believe that mass surveillance has come too far under the Bush and Obama administrations. We call upon our leaders in technology companies to improve security and implement strong encryption to protect our digital lives from the rising threat of foreign hackers and domestic surveillance.
